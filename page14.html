Do you know what your a11y problems are?

Identify Accessibility Problems: "Do you know what your A11Y problems are?" If no, proceed to "Did you evaluate the product?" If yes, move to "Are your findings prioritised?" Product Evaluation: "Did you evaluate the product?" If no, proceed to "How many pages do you have?" If yes, return to the main flow asking "Are your findings prioritised?" Page Count: "How many pages do you have?" If less than 5, "Test all pages." If more than 5, "Test frequently used key processes." Prioritization of Findings: "Are your findings prioritised?" If no, "Start with crucial processes, frequently-used web pages, high impact repairs, etc." If yes, proceed to "Fix them." Fixing Issues: After testing or prioritizing, the next step is to "Fix them." Post-Fix Evaluation: "Do you still have known issues?" If yes, "Report known barriers." If no, the process ends. Conclusion: The flowchart concludes with the statement: "There is no 100% compliance with A11Y guidelines. But always aim as high as possible."
