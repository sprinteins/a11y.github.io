How can adaptive strategies help?

Adaptive strategies can help coping with hard to access content and digital services.

  1. Text Zoom

    Makes text easier to read and increases understanding.

  2. Captions

    Displays speech and auditory information of a video.

  3. High Contrast Mode

    Helps people to better distinguish content.

  4. Personal Configurations

    Helps people to fit the interaction to their needs.
